    Procedure:
  • Step 1: For the given monomial compute all the check sets.
  • Step 2: For every checkset, compute the XOR of the received vector bits, who's positions are in the check set.
  • Step 3: Sum of those XORs would result in a 1, some in a 0. Pick the majority.
  • Step 4: The observations panel would let you know if your choice is wrong. If it correct, move on to the next monomial.
  • Step 5: Press "Reload" to reload the page and try a new example.
